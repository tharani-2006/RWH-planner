## 💰 Cost Calculation Workflow

```mermaid
graph TD
    CostStart[💰 Cost Calculation Start] --> BaseData[📊 Get Base Parameters]
    BaseData --> |Volume, Structure Type, Goal| Dataset[📋 Load Cost Dataset]
    
    Dataset --> Material{🏗️ Material Selection}
    
    Material --> |Storage| Tank[🔵 Tank Costs]
    Material --> |Recharge| Structure[🟤 Structure Costs]
    
    Tank --> |Plastic/Cement/Metal| TankCalc[💵 Calculate Tank Cost]
    Structure --> |Pit/Trench/Shaft| StructCalc[💵 Calculate Structure Cost]
    
    TankCalc --> Multipliers[⚡ Apply Multipliers]
    StructCalc --> Multipliers
    
    Multipliers --> Soil{🌍 Soil Type}
    Soil --> |Sandy| Easy[1.0x Easy Digging]
    Soil --> |Clayey| Medium[1.5x Medium Difficulty]
    Soil --> |Rocky| Hard[2.0x Hard Digging]
    
    Easy --> Space{📏 Space Factor}
    Medium --> Space
    Hard --> Space
    
    Space --> |Large >25m²| SpaceEasy[1.0x Easy Access]
    Space --> |Medium 10-25m²| SpaceMed[1.1x Medium Access]
    Space --> |Small <10m²| SpaceHard[1.3x Difficult Access]
    
    SpaceEasy --> RoofEff{🏠 Roof Efficiency}
    SpaceMed --> RoofEff
    SpaceHard --> RoofEff
    
    RoofEff --> |Non-Absorptive| Eff95[95% Collection]
    RoofEff --> |Absorptive| Eff75[75% Collection]
    
    Eff95 --> Final[💰 Final Cost Calculation]
    Eff75 --> Final
    
    Final --> Breakdown[📊 Cost Breakdown]
    Breakdown --> |Material + Labor + Contingency| Payback[📈 Payback Analysis]
    
    Payback --> Result[📋 Cost Result]
```
